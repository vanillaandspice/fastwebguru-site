## Product Overview

**One-liner:**
Cortex is Fast Web Guru's $30/month AI ecosystem — three pillars bundled together that turn your hosting into an intelligent, self-executing digital business asset. Available as a complete bundle only.

**What it does:**
Cortex is a bundled add-on to any FWG hosting plan that delivers three AI-powered layers working together: AI-native hosting infrastructure that you can edit by talking to it (Host Systems), a custom Business DNA file + dedicated AI Specialist who actually knows your brand (Team Stack), and 20 bespoke growth workflows built specifically for your business to work through with your team over the coming months — with more workflows and team workshops added regularly (Ops Grid). Together, they transform a passive website and hosting account into an active, AI-driven business system.

**The $30 bundle:**
Cortex Host Systems + Cortex Team Stack + Cortex Ops Grid = $30/month (bundle only — all three pillars included)

**Sub-brand concept:**
"A unified, cognitive ecosystem that transforms standard web hosting into a living, executing business asset."

**Previous name:**
AI Extender (renamed and restructured — formerly $20/month with no defined pillars)

**Product category:**
AI business infrastructure add-on / managed AI ecosystem / done-with-you AI service

**Product type:**
Monthly subscription add-on. Sits on top of any FWG hosting tier. Included by default with the $240/mo Advanced plan.

**Business model:**
$30/month complete bundle — all three pillars included. Not sold separately.

One-line pitch: **"Your hosting just grew a brain."**

## Problems & Pain Points

**Core problem:**
Their website is passive infrastructure — it sits on a server and waits. Generic AI tools don't know their business and produce off-brand output. Every "strategy" they receive from an agency or consultant dies because there's no ongoing system to actually execute it.

**Why alternatives fall short:**
- **ChatGPT / Gemini / Jasper** — powerful but generic; don't know the business; no integration with the website; no execution layer; owner left alone to figure it out
- **Traditional AI agencies** — expensive; project-based; produce strategy docs without owning the execution; no ongoing infrastructure
- **DIY AI setup** — requires technical skill, prompt expertise, and hours the owner doesn't have; results are inconsistent without brand training
- **Standard hosting add-ons** — infrastructure only; no intelligence layer; no human oversight

**What it costs them (if they don't upgrade):**
- Their AI-savvy competitors are already running workflows they haven't started
- Every month without Cortex is a month of unexecuted growth ideas
- Their Brand DNA doesn't exist anywhere — every piece of AI output has to start from scratch
- The website continues to be a cost centre, not a growth asset

**Emotional tension:**
FOMO meets paralysis. They know the third major shift in business infrastructure is happening (PCs → Internet → AI). They don't want to be the person who missed it. But every "AI solution" they've looked at either costs too much, requires too much learning, or produces garbage output. Cortex is the bridge between knowing AI matters and actually having AI working in their business.

## Competitive Landscape

**Direct competitors:**
- AI hosting add-ons from traditional hosts (WP Engine AI, etc.) — infrastructure-only; no brand DNA ingestion; no human specialist; no workflow execution layer
- AI chatbot add-ons (Tidio, Intercom AI, etc.) — customer service tools only; don't generate growth workflows or ingest business DNA

**Secondary competitors (different solution, same problem):**
- Generic AI tools (ChatGPT, Gemini, Jasper, Claude direct) — fall short because: no brand DNA training, no integration with the website, no execution layer, no human oversight, owner is left alone to manage and prompt
- "AI strategy" from marketing agencies — fall short because: expensive, project-based, no ongoing infrastructure, no execution accountability

**Indirect competitors (conflicting approach):**
- DIY approach ("I'll figure out AI myself") — the most common competitor. Falls short because: takes months; without brand DNA training and workflow templates, results are inconsistent; no specialist to review output
- "Wait and see" inertia — cheapest option in the short term; most expensive in opportunity cost

**The moat:**
No competitor bundles AI-native infrastructure + brand DNA training + human specialist + executable workflows for $30/month as a complete package. The $30 bundle is the clearest value equation in the market.

## Brand Voice (Cortex)

**Tone:**
Same as FWG overall — like a knowledgeable mate who tells it straight. For Cortex specifically: slightly more confident and forward-leaning (Cortex is FWG's growth story), but never arrogant. The $30 math does the heavy lifting — let the numbers speak.

**Style:**
Punchy. Short. Numbers-led (the $30 bundle price is the hook — use it). Concrete over abstract. "20 growth workflows built for your business" beats "a comprehensive workflow solution." Avoid all AI buzzwords that make business owners' eyes glaze over.

**Personality:**
Technically sharp but unpretentious. The friend who's deeply into AI but explains it in plain English because they genuinely want you to understand it, not because they want to impress you.

**Cortex-specific voice notes:**
