## What Changed from v5 — Platform & Hosting Clarity

The goal of v6 is to make it unmistakable that **Fast Web Guru builds and hosts on every platform** — WordPress, Headless WordPress, and Full Code — without diluting the full-code AI flagship. Cortex capability *scales with the platform*, and ecommerce pricing is now explicit.

| Change | Pages affected | Rationale |
|--------|---------------|-----------|
| **NEW** "Every platform. One price." model — WordPress (Standard or WooCommerce), Headless WordPress, Full Code (FWG-native) all host at the same $40 core price | Pricing, Core plan, Homepage, Cortex page, Website Builds | Visitors with existing sites need to know we support their stack before they'll consider us |
| **NEW** Ecommerce hosting tier — **$95/mo, Cortex included** (replaces legacy Woo tiers $75/$95) | Pricing, Core plan, Homepage | Ecommerce was previously only visible via Advanced; now a clear standalone hosting price |
| Cortex capability now framed as **platform-dependent** (full power on Full Code, scaling down on Headless/WordPress) — replaces the binary "any host / FWG hosting" framing | Cortex page capability table, Pricing add-on, Core plan | Honest about ability variance without diluting the full-code concept |
| Core hosting line reworded from "full-code hosting (not a page builder)" → "hosting on the platform that fits you" with full-code positioned as the flagship tier | Pricing, Core plan, Homepage | Stops alienating WordPress/existing-site visitors while keeping full-code aspirational |
| Website Builds: explicit "we build in any framework" messaging added | Website Builds page, Pricing builds block, Homepage | New customers ordering sites need to know they can have WordPress/legacy tech too |
| Pricing FAQ: added platform + ecommerce-pricing questions | Pricing | Pre-empts the #1 question from existing-site owners |

**Unchanged from v5:** Cortex three-pillar structure and pricing ($30 add-on), Advanced ($240, Cortex included), AI Extender, How It Works, About, Partners, Contact, HITL, individual Cortex pillar pages, Global CTA component.

**Spelling note:** This master copy uses **Cortex** throughout, consistent with the FWG brand DNA and v1–v5. (Brief uses "Coretex" — kept as **Cortex** for brand consistency; flag if a respell is intended.)

Cortex ($30/mo more) adds three AI pillars: AI-native hosting with Talk-to-Edit, a Business DNA workshop that trains an AI brain on your specific brand, 20 bespoke growth workflows to work through with your team (more added regularly, plus workshops to skill up your leaders), and 1 hour of dedicated AI Specialist time. If you want AI actively working for your business — not just in the background — Cortex is the upgrade. Advanced clients get Cortex automatically at no extra charge.

**Q: What exactly is Cortex?**
Cortex is FWG's AI ecosystem add-on — three pillars at $10 each. Host Systems upgrades your hosting to AI-native infrastructure with Talk-to-Edit. Team Stack builds your Business DNA and trains a personalised AI brain on it (plus 1 hr/mo AI Specialist). Ops Grid gives you 20 bespoke growth workflows to work through with your team — more added regularly, plus workshops to build capability. [See the full Cortex page →](/cortex)

**Q: Can I get Cortex without being on FWG hosting?**
Yes. Cortex Team Stack (Brand DNA + AI brain + Specialist) and Cortex Ops Grid (20 workflows) work on any website, on any host. Host Systems' Talk-to-Edit capability runs at full power on FWG's AI-native infrastructure. If you're on another host, you get the intelligence and execution pillars immediately — Host Systems upgrades when you move to FWG hosting.

**Q: I already have a WordPress site — do I have to rebuild it?**
No. We host and run WordPress sites (standard or WooCommerce) as-is, at the same $40 core price as any other platform. You keep the familiar editing you already know, and we handle updates, backups, security, and support. If you ever want a faster, more AI-native front end, we can move you to a headless or full-code build without changing providers. [See how platforms compare →](/pricing#platforms)

**Q: What platforms can you build and host on?**
Three: **WordPress** (standard or WooCommerce), **Headless WordPress** (a full-code front end on your WordPress content), and **Full Code** (our AI-native flagship build). All three host at the same $40 core price. The difference is how much of Cortex's live AI site-editing runs on top — full power on full-code, scaling down on headless and WordPress. Cortex's brain and workflows run fully on every platform.

**Q: How much is ecommerce, and what does it include?**
Ecommerce hosting is **$95/month with Cortex AI included** — whether your store runs on WooCommerce or a full-code build. It's everything in Core, tuned for store traffic and checkout reliability, with product, stock, and order support built into your same-day updates. This replaced our old separate WooCommerce hosting tiers, so there's now one clear price for any store. If you need a full team managing the store every week, that's Advanced ($240/mo).

**Q: Is $40/month the real price, or does it go up later?**
$40/month is the real price. It doesn't increase after 12 months. It doesn't have a "renewal rate." Month-to-month plans cost $40/month. That's it.

**Q: Is there a minimum commitment?**
No minimum. Month one is month one. You can cancel at the end of any month with no fee and no questions.

**Q: What if I need more than 1 hour of support per month?**
You can purchase additional hours at our standard rate, or upgrade to Advanced. Annual clients can also pool hours across the year — so if you need 3 hours in January and none in February, that's fine.

**Q: What happens to unused hours?**
Monthly hours are use-it-or-lose-it each calendar month. Annual plans pool for the year — use them in any combination.

**Q: Do I own my website and domain?**
Yes. Your site is yours. Your domain is yours. You get full login access to everything from day one. If you ever leave, you take it all with you. Nothing is held back.

**Q: What does "same-day update" actually mean?**
Comment on your website by 4pm and it'll be done same day. We're available 6am–10pm, including weekends.

**Q: What happens if the internet goes down?**
Your site keeps running. We've engineered our infrastructure for exactly this — three complete copies of your website run simultaneously, with automatic failover. The system operates at 90%+ capacity even through a power outage or on mobile-only connectivity. For regional businesses especially, this isn't a bonus feature — it's the reason we built it this way.
