# Fast Web Guru — Launch Plan
## Corey Haines Launch Framework (ORB)

*Created: 2026-06-07 | Source: product-marketing.md + launch skill*

---

## Launch Philosophy

FWG is launching a rebuilt brand on an existing client base, in specific geographic markets, with a community-led distribution model (advocates). This is not a Product Hunt tech launch — it's a local-first, personal, word-of-mouth launch amplified by digital channels.

The ORB framework applies:
- **Owned:** Email list (existing 20 clients), website, assessment tool
- **Rented:** Facebook (AU small business groups), Instagram, LinkedIn
- **Borrowed:** Fenwick Hill Group network, local business networks, advocate Red Envelope campaign

---

## Phase 1: Internal Launch (Pre-launch — Week 1–2)

**Goal:** Validate the assessment tool and onboarding flow with known contacts before opening publicly.

**Actions:**
- Run 5 test assessments manually on known websites (team members, FHG businesses)
- Identify any gaps in the assessment report template
- Test the email delivery, confirmation page, and nurture sequence
- Confirm BugHerd + Workflow Hub onboarding works end-to-end
- Have at least 1 person complete the full flow from assessment → $40/mo signup → Day 7

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Assessment form live (Tally or similar)
- [ ] Thank you page live
- [ ] Email 1 (assessment delivery) triggers correctly
- [ ] Emails 2–5 are loaded and scheduled correctly
- [ ] $40/mo signup flow works
- [ ] Welcome email triggers on signup
- [ ] Support person introduction flow tested
- [ ] BugHerd installs successfully on a test site

---

## Phase 2: Existing Client Activation (Week 2–3)

**Goal:** Upgrade the existing 20 legacy WordPress hosting clients. These are the highest-quality first conversions — they already trust FWG.

**The email (from Steve personally, not automated):**

**Subject:** [First Name] — we rebuilt Fast Web Guru. Here's your free AI assessment.

> Hi [First Name],
>
> You've been a Fast Web Guru client for [time]. I wanted you to be the first to know what we've been building.
>
> We've rebuilt the whole thing from scratch — new tech stack, AI tools, a dedicated support person model, and same-day website updates via a tool called BugHerd.
>
> Before I say anything else, I'd like to give you a free AI assessment of [website] — a proper look at where your site stands today and what the AI era means for your business specifically.
>
> [Get your free AI assessment →]
>
> No catch. You've been with us for years and I want to make sure we're actually serving you well, not just keeping the lights on.
>
> If you're curious about what we're building, happy to talk — just reply to this email.
>
> Steve
> Fast Web Guru

**Follow-up:** Any client who doesn't open within 5 days → one follow-up. Any client who opens but doesn't click → personal phone call from Steve.

**Upgrade offer:** Existing clients who move to the new Core plan in the first 30 days get their first month at the legacy rate (or free — confirm with Steve).

---

## Phase 3: Soft Launch — Assessment Tool Goes Live (Week 3–4)

**Goal:** First external traffic, refine the assessment process, collect first non-client testimonials.

**Actions:**

**Website goes live** with the assessment CTA on all pages.

**Fenwick Hill Group network activation:**
- Email to Fenwick Hill Group business contacts (food, retail, hospitality — perfect target market)
- Personal outreach from Steve to 10 priority contacts
- Ask for one thing: "Try the assessment and tell me honestly if it's useful"

**Local business group outreach (Hunter Valley first):**
- Post in Hunter Valley small business Facebook groups: "We built a free AI website assessment for Aussie small businesses — happy to share if anyone wants a look."
- Natural, community-first tone. Not promotional. Genuine offer to help.
- Respond to every comment personally

**LinkedIn post (Steve's personal account):**
> "After 10+ years managing websites for small businesses, we rebuilt Fast Web Guru from the ground up.
>
> New tech stack. AI tools. A dedicated human support person. Same-day updates from $40/month.
>
> We built a free AI website assessment to show what's possible — and to help businesses figure out where they actually stand.
>
> [Link to assessment]
>
> If you've got a small business in Australia and you want an honest look at your website, it takes 30 seconds."

---

## Phase 4: Advocate / Red Envelope Rollout (Week 4–6)

**Goal:** Activate the first wave of advocates in the initial priority regions.

**Priority order:** Hunter Valley → Newcastle → Sydney → Melbourne → Yarra Valley

**Advocate activation sequence:**
1. Identify 3–5 candidates in each city (from FHG network, LinkedIn, referrals)
2. Personal outreach from Steve: "I'd like to talk about the Fast Web Guru partner program"
3. 30-min call to walk through the model, commissions, and the Red Envelope campaign
4. Onboard interested advocates: give them FWG plan access, introduce them to the support team, provide their Red Envelope materials
5. First advocate Red Envelope campaigns go out in each city

**Red Envelope campaign targeting:**
- Local food businesses, hospitality, retail — the sector FHG knows best
- Hand-delivered or courier where possible
- Personalised to the business (the advocate visits, spends 20 mins, knows something specific about them)
- Contains: custom AI assessment snapshot for their business (pre-done by the FWG team), handwritten note, local gift
- No prices, no selling. Just: "We wanted to show you what's possible for a business like yours."

---

## Phase 5: Full Launch (Week 6–8)

**Goal:** Open the floodgates. Maximum visibility.

**Channel actions:**

**Email:**
- Announcement to full list (all assessments + clients + contacts)
- Subject: "Fast Web Guru is officially open."
- Simple, direct, Australian voice

**Blog post:**
- Title: "We rebuilt Fast Web Guru. Here's why."
- The full story: what we inherited, what we built, what we believe
- Ends with assessment CTA
- Published and shared across all channels

**Social:**
- Instagram: 3-post launch sequence (what it is / how it works / the offer)
- Facebook: Share to AU small business groups in each priority city
- LinkedIn: Steve's personal post + Fast Web Guru company page

**PR angle (optional but strong):**
- "Australian tech startup gives SMBs enterprise-grade AI web support from $40/month"
- Pitch to SmartCompany, StartupSmart, BRW, local Hunter Valley / Newcastle media
- The charity/cooperative angle (VS Creative) is a genuine human interest story

**Referral activation:**
- Email to first 20 paying clients: "Know a business that could use this? We'd love a referral — and we'll take good care of them."
