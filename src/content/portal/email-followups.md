# Fast Web Guru — Email Nurture Sequence
## Assessment Lead → $40/mo Client

*Created: 2026-06-07 | Source: product-marketing.md + emails skill*
*Voice: Conversational Australian — warm, direct, not corporate. Reads like a real person.*

---

## Sequence Overview

```
Sequence name:   FWG Assessment Nurture
Trigger:         Assessment form submitted
Goal:            Convert to $40/mo Core plan or book a call
Length:          5 emails over 14 days
Exit condition:  Signs up for any paid plan OR books a call
Email tool:      Mailchimp / Customer.io / Kit (TBD)
Personalisation: [First Name], [website URL], [business type] from form
```

## Email 3: The Real Story

**Send:** Day 5
**Job:** Tell the story of why FWG exists. Build emotional connection. Subtle credibility.

---

**Subject:** Why we rebuilt Fast Web Guru from scratch

**Preview:** Honest version — not the PR version.

---

Hi [First Name],

Most "about us" pages are rubbish. Ours probably will be too, so let me just tell you directly.

For over a decade, Fast Web Guru kept websites running for small businesses. Good businesses, run by good people — who kept getting treated badly by the web industry.

Developers who charged $150 an hour to change a price. Agencies that sent generic reports and called it a strategy. AI tools that produced content that could have been written by anyone, for anyone.

We watched it happen for years. And when we took over the business and rebuilt it, we had one goal: build the service we wished existed when we were small business owners ourselves.

That meant:
- Same-day updates, not next-week estimates
- One fixed price, not invoices for every change
- A real human who knows your business — not a ticket queue
- AI that actually does something — not another login and dashboard to manage

We're not perfect. We're newer at some of this than we'd like to be. But we're genuinely building something different, and we're doing it with real businesses as our test cases — not in a lab.

If that sounds like what you've been looking for, [have a look at what's included in our Core plan].

And if you've got questions before you do anything, just reply. I read every email.

[Name]
Fast Web Guru

- Ecommerce → quick win is product descriptions / abandoned cart
- Hospitality → quick win is Google Business Profile / review automation
- Trade/services → quick win is enquiry capture / follow-up automation
- Professional services → quick win is social proof placement / testimonial gathering
